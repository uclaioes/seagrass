About Us

We are a passionate team of UCLA students researching the full breadth of benefits that marine vegetation, such as kelp forest and seagrass beds, provide for our changing environment. As part of UCLA'S Institute of the Environment and Sustainability, we wish to establish a scientific communications portal for future students & researchers.
